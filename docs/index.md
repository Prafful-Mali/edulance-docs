## Project Purpose
**Edulance** provides an online space for students, developers, and creators to connect and collaborate on **group projects** or **hackathons**.  
It addresses the real-world problem of finding suitable teammates for technical projects by matching people based on their skills and project requirements.

---

## Problem Statement
Students in colleges often struggle to find a good group of teammates within their class or campus.  
As a result, it becomes difficult for them to participate in hackathons or group projects at a competitive level.  

---

## Proposed Solution
- Create project posts with clear details such as title, description, people required, and required skills.  
- Join or apply to open posts that match their interests and expertise.  
- Collaborate effectively with others to build meaningful projects and gain real-world experience.  

---

## Target Audience
- Students participating in hackathons or college projects.  
- Independent developers looking to collaborate on side projects.  
- Early-stage entrepreneurs seeking technical collaborators.  

---

## Business Impact

| Metric | Impact |
|--------|---------|
| **User Engagement** | Encourages collaboration by increasing interactions and participation in projects. |
| **Skill Development** | Helps users enhance their technical and teamwork skills through real-world collaboration. |
| **Scalability** | Can evolve into a community-driven platform with project recommendations, leaderboards, and achievements. |
